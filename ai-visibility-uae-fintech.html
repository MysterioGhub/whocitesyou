AI visibility in UAE fintech: who gets recommended

Audits of three UAE fintech brands show three very different positions: category dominance, a high-intent blind spot and visibility that changes sharply by engine.

Standout findings

Agreement at the top, blind spots underneath

Category control

The Tabby and Tamara duopoly

Every engine placed Tabby first on the flagship UAE BNPL query. Tamara was the top competitor by share of voice, while Postpay, Cashew and Spotii trailed.

Use-case gap

Sarwa vanished on the expat index-fund query

ChatGPT and Perplexity both answered without naming Sarwa. Gemini did name it, so the blind spot was large but not universal.

Engine split

Ziina missed its payment-link moment in two engines

ChatGPT listed competing providers without Ziina, and Perplexity named Mamo instead. Gemini included Ziina behind Stripe.

Cross-engine lesson

One overall score can hide different answer surfaces

Tabby had broad agreement across engines. Sarwa and Ziina each had a query where Gemini included the brand while ChatGPT and Perplexity did not.
