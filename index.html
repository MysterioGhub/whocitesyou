Methodology

How the audit works

This is generative engine optimization (GEO / AEO) done as measurement, not guesswork. The process is the same for every client, so scores are comparable and defensible.

STEP 01

We map your query matrix

12 questions a real buyer would ask about your category — split across category, comparison, alternative, and use-case intent. These are the moments where AI hands out recommendations.

STEP 02

We run every query, on every engine, repeatedly

ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity, with repeated trials per query. AI answers are distributions, not facts — so we sample them like distributions to get a stable read instead of one lucky (or unlucky) screenshot.

STEP 03

The pipeline scores and classifies every capture

Each answer is automatically classified — cited, invisible, or misrepresented — with mention strength and which competitors were named instead. A human then verifies the scoring before anything ships.

STEP 04

Your software-generated report is delivered

An overall visibility score, per-engine breakdown, competitor share-of-voice, and a prioritized set of recommendations — generated as a clean HTML report and delivered digitally within 5 business days.

FAQ

Questions, answered

What is a GEO / AEO audit, in plain terms?

GEO (generative engine optimization) and AEO (answer engine optimization) are about being visible in AI-generated answers, the way SEO was about ranking on Google. An AI visibility audit measures how often and how favorably AI engines cite your brand when your customers ask — and shows you how to improve it. That's exactly what we do.

Why repeated trials instead of a single screenshot?

Because AI answers are distributions, not fixed facts. Ask the same question twice and you can get different brands, in a different order. A one-off answer tells you almost nothing. We run each query multiple times, across three engines, so your citation rate and your competitors' share of voice reflect what typically happens — not one lucky roll.

Which engines do you cover?

ChatGPT, Google Gemini, and Perplexity today. Anthropic's Claude and Google's AI Overviews are on the roadmap. If AI answers matter for how buyers find you, these are the surfaces that decide whether you're cited.

What exactly do I receive, and how fast?

A self-contained HTML report: an overall AI visibility score, a per-engine breakdown, your cited / invisible / misrepresented queries, competitor share-of-voice, and a prioritized fix list. Delivered within 5 business days of us starting your audit. The two sample reports on this page are exactly the format you'll get.
