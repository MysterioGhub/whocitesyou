Prioritized actions to close the gaps above.

Turn growth how-to queries into product surface: 'How to grow a newsletter from 0 to 10,000 subscribers' yields only a passing tool mention on Gemini ('platforms like Beehiiv or SparkLoop') and none on ChatGPT/Perplexity — even though Perplexity cites beehiiv's own growth blog as an authority without naming the product. Restructure top growth-blog posts so the referral program, Boosts, and recommendation network are named as the mechanism, not generic advice.
  3. Defend the #1 category position with monitoring, not assumptions: beehiiv is the top or first-listed recommendation in 17 of 24 competitive query×engine cells (e.g. 'strongest option on the market' — Gemini; 'my default recommendation' — ChatGPT; 'strongest all-around pick' — Perplexity). This dominance is sourced from third-party 2026 roundups; track those source articles and re-test quarterly, because a single influential roundup update can flip multiple engine answers at once.

Publish one canonical, crawler-friendly pricing page (FAQ/schema markup, explicit annual-vs-monthly figures) so AI answers stop presenting inconsistent prices next to competitor comparisons.

This is beehiiv's strongest AI-visible message — reinforce it with an interactive break-even calculator page engines can cite, and extend the same math to Kit and Ghost comparisons where it is currently absent.

7  Methodology & limitations

Engines tested. ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity.

Query matrix. 12 queries spanning brand-direct, trust, category, comparison, alternative, use-case and pricing intents, reflecting how real buyers ask AI tools about Newsletter platforms for creators & media.

Capture. Each query was run in a fresh conversation with no prior context, and the full answer stored verbatim as the evidence layer of this report.

Scoring. Visibility = 100 × (0.5 × mention rate + 0.3 × average recommendation strength / 3 + 0.2 × first-place rate), computed over competitive-intent queries. Brand-direct, trust and pricing queries almost always name the brand, so they are reported separately and excluded from the competitive score.

Limitations. AI answers vary between runs and over time. These results are a representative sample captured on July 8, 2026, not a census. Treat scores as directional and re-audit periodically to track movement.
